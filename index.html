    About Us

    Engineering Solution Services is a premier engineering company with over 7 years of experience in manufacturing engineering. Located in Ghardaia, Algeria, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and innovation. We collaborate closely with esteemed Chinese and Turkish companies, extending our engineering services to North Africa

    Our Mission

    make engineering simple and highly qualified by harnessing the latest and most advanced global technologies ,also pioneering the development of the Algerian and African engineering landscape.

    Our Plan

    Our plan is to streamline complex engineering processes, ensuring accessibility to the most advanced technologies while contributing to the growth and excellence of engineering in Algeria and across the African continent.

    Our Vision

    to be the leading engineering solutions provider in Algeria and beyond. We aim to continually innovate and adapt to emerging technologies, setting the industry standard for customer satisfaction and reliability.

    Our Services

    Automation Services (Specialized in Siemens)

    Our expertise in automation includes PLC line production, HMI (Human Machine Interface) solutions, programming, and troubleshooting.

    Subcontracting for Industrial Projects

    Partner with us for reliable and efficient subcontracting services.

    Fabrication Machines

    Our services extend to fabricating machines, from 3D design with animated simulations to the realization of the machine. We bring your ideas to life with precision and innovation.

    Providing Machine Parts

    We offer a comprehensive range of high-quality machine parts to keep your operations running smoothly.

    After-sales Services

    We provide exceptional post-purchase support to maximize the value of your investments.

    Importing Machinery

    We source the best supplier and import machinery to meet your industrial needs with providing installation and after selling services.

    Why Choose Us

    We offer a comprehensive range of engineering services, from design and planning to construction and project management. This means that we can provide you with a one-stop solution for all of your engineering needs
